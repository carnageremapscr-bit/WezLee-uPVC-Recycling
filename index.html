About WezLee

We focus on giving uPVC materials a second life. From windows and doors to offcuts and frames, our process keeps recyclable material in circulation.

We work with installers, fabricators, contractors, and homeowners who want a cleaner and more responsible disposal route. Every collection is designed to be practical for busy sites and straightforward for domestic customers.

Whether you are a homeowner replacing windows, a trade installer clearing regular offcuts, or a commercial site managing larger volumes, WezLee provides a practical recycling route.

If you have regular waste output, WezLee can help you build a repeat pickup plan that fits your workflow and improves recycling consistency across projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you collect from trade sites?

Yes. We support installers, contractors, and fabrication businesses with regular or one-off collections depending on your volume.

Can homeowners use your service?

Absolutely. Domestic customers can enquire about suitable drop-off or collection options for smaller projects.

What should be removed before pickup?

Keep loads as clean as possible and separate non-uPVC materials where practical. If in doubt, send photos before booking and we will guide you.

Do you handle recurring collections?

Yes. We can set up recurring pickup schedules for businesses that generate regular uPVC waste streams.
